Studyship Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Studyship ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Studyship mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name or username
  • Profile information provided by you

User Content

  • Profile photos
  • Profile descriptions
  • Messages exchanged with other users
  • Study preferences and interests

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may send push notifications related to app activity and account updates.

How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable matching and social features
  • Facilitate messaging between users
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain platform security

Data Storage

Studyship uses third-party infrastructure providers, including Supabase, to securely store and process user data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

  • When required by law
  • To protect the safety and security of users
  • With service providers necessary to operate the app

User-Generated Content

Information you choose to include in your profile may be visible to other users of the app.

Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion by contacting us using the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Studyship is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
